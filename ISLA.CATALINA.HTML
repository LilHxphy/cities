ISLA CATALINA

Isla Catalina is a city located in the Dominican Republic. The island was named "Santa Catalina by Christopher Columbus in May, 1494. It is a popular destination for tourism right next to La Romana.

Many of the tourist ships depart from La Romana and go straight to Isla Catalina. The city has nice white sand with blue crystal clear water. Many go for the nice coral reefs that are close to shore.
